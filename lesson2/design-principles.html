PARC Proximity

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints

thechurchofjesuschrist.org

Description
In the website above, The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints utilizes proximity beautifully to optimize the user experience.

The space in the grid-gap helps separate the different talks, articles, and videos so users can clearly distinguish between them.In addition, the proximity between the images and the title is within the div element, which helps give context to the picture.

For example, it is enjoyable while browsing that the title is over the image when it is a talk, but when it is a news article, the title is below the picture. This proximity also separates what kind of information you will be looking at when you click the image.

PARC Contrast

Mondly

mondly.com

Description
Mondly has a clear contrast on its website, an engaging color palette. I love how the text is white, the background a light blue, and the outline colors of each icon are different yet contrast well with its environment.

The colors in the menu icons had a white outline with a background that matched the rest of the screen background. This strategy helps give subtleness to the menu options and emphasizes the icons in the center of the screen.

The crown icon stands out boldly in the top right corner without taking away from the website. The dark blue on the icons on the right side helps to contrast the background but helps other icons stand out.

White Space and Clean Design

Duolingo

duolingo.com

Description
Duolingo demonstrates that less is more. The white space and clean design help so that you aren't distracted by other colors or designs on the page.

This decluttered technique helps emphasize the icons displayed. Some menu options have a subtle grey outline, the same background color as the rest. On the menu options at the bottom of the screen, the active link has color while the rest has none.

This method helps you know which page you are on without title text. The navbar on the top has smaller icons that help make the site visually pleasing without emphasizing so much color.
